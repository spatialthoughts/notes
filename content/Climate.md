# Climate

## 2026

- [MausamNow](https://open.substack.com/pub/abhijitvaidya/p/your-weather-app-wasnt-built-for): A Progressive Web App that synthesizes forecasts from multiple weather models (GFS, ECMWF, IMD) alongside radar and satellite data to deliver hyperlocal rain predictions for Indian cities — showing which localities will be affected, intensity, and trend. [Keywords: `weather` `India` `hyperlocal` `monsoon` `forecasting` `GFS` `ECMWF` `IMD`]

## 2025

- [Unprecedented Continental Drying](https://www.science.org/doi/10.1126/sciadv.adx0298): Science Advances paper documenting unprecedented continental drying, shrinking freshwater availability, and increasing land contributions to sea level rise globally. [Keywords: `climate` `freshwater` `drought` `sea level rise` `hydrology`]

## 2024

- [Aurora: Microsoft AI Foundation Model for Weather](https://github.com/microsoft/aurora): Microsoft's AI foundation model for atmospheric forecasting — pre-trained on large datasets and adaptable to weather prediction, air quality, and ocean wave modeling. [Keywords: `climate` `AI` `foundation model` `weather` `ERA5` `atmospheric forecasting`]

## Earlier

- [Global Sea Level Change Indicators 1950-2050](https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu/cdsapp#!/dataset/sis-water-level-change-indicators-cmip6?tab=overview): Copernicus Climate Data Store dataset of sea level change indicators derived from reanalysis and high-resolution CMIP6 projections. [Keywords: `climate` `sea level` `CMIP6` `Copernicus` `reanalysis`]

- [Germany 100 Years of Temperature Change](https://nellomaps.com/2022/08/06/small-multiple-map/): Small multiple map visualization showing 100 years of temperature change in Germany. [Keywords: `climate` `temperature` `visualization` `small multiples` `cartography` `Germany`]

- [Absolute Temperatures vs Anomalies](https://www.ncei.noaa.gov/access/monitoring/dyk/anomalies-vs-temperature): NOAA explanation of the difference between absolute temperatures and temperature anomalies in climate data. [Keywords: `climate` `temperature` `anomalies` `NOAA` `data`]

- [Global Drought Monitoring with DSI in GEE](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00704-021-03715-9): Paper on global drought monitoring using the Drought Severity Index in Google Earth Engine. [Keywords: `climate` `drought` `DSI` `GEE` `global monitoring`]
