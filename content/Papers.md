# Papers

## 2026

- [Learning with Less: Label-Efficient Land Cover Classification](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2511.03004): Paper on label-efficient land cover classification at very high spatial resolution using self-supervised deep learning. *deep learning, self-supervised, land cover, label efficiency, remote sensing*

- [Embeddings as Products](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.13134): Paper on using embeddings as product representations in geospatial contexts. *embeddings, machine learning, geospatial*

- [Sampling for Land Cover](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S003442572600026X): Paper on sampling strategies for land cover classification using remote sensing. *land cover, sampling, remote sensing, classification*

## Earlier

- [Change Detection Techniques](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/235245895_Change_Detection_Techniques): Review paper on remote sensing change detection techniques and methodologies. *change detection, remote sensing, satellite imagery*

- [Zone Builder - FOSS4G 2021](https://github.com/zonebuilders/zonebuilder): Tool for building urban zones for transport and planning analysis. *urban planning, zoning, transport, GIS*

- [Users of Open Big Earth Data Analysis](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0098300421002077): Analysis of current state and users of open Big Earth Observation data. *GEE, big data, Earth observation, open data*

- [Overview of Platforms for Big Earth Observation Data](https://www.mdpi.com/2072-4292/12/8/1253/htm): MDPI review of major platforms for big Earth observation data management and analysis. *GEE, big data, cloud computing, Earth observation*

- [Trend Analysis: Revival of Indian Summer Monsoon Rainfall since 2002](https://www.nature.com/articles/nclimate3348): Nature Climate Change paper on the revival trend of Indian summer monsoon rainfall from 2002 onwards. *climate, rainfall, monsoon, India, trend analysis, GEE*

- [Triangle Method for Soil Moisture Estimation](https://www.isprs.org/proceedings/XXXVII/congress/8_pdf/3_WG-VIII-3/13.pdf): ISPRS paper on the Triangle Method for soil moisture estimation using dry and wet edges of satellite-derived feature spaces. *soil moisture, estimation, remote sensing, triangle method, satellite*

- [Color Ramp for Elevation Maps](https://cartographicperspectives.org/index.php/journal/article/download/cp69-patterson-jenny/pdf): Paper on designing effective color ramps for elevation visualization in cartography. *cartography, color ramp, elevation, DEM, visualization*

- [Suspended Particulate Matter Analysis - COVID Lockdown](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s00128-022-03638-9): Paper on suspended particulate matter analysis before and during COVID lockdown using GEE at Ukai Reservoir. *GEE, water quality, suspended particulate matter, COVID, air quality*
